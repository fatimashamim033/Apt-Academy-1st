About APT ACADEMY & OBJECTIVES


Welcome to APT Academy, where education knows no bounds! With a team of five committed teachers, we cater to a diverse range of students, from Class 1 to college level. Our aim extends beyond tutoring, we aspire to evolve into a full-fledged school, offering a comprehensive educational journey. Additionally, envisioning the future, we aim to introduce computer classes, providing holistic and modern learning experiences for our students. Join us in this transformative educational adventure at APT Academy.
